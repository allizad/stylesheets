my name is alli

I'm a local.

I became a high school teacher straight out of college with teach for america. I taught Calculus, coached volleyball, directed Shakespeare, and mixed chemicals for Chemistry class. In the winter of 2013 I decided to make a career pivot. Enter makersquare and developer bootcamp.

I am so excited about so many things: about people and the creative process and how invaluable strong, honest leadership is for any company or organization. I love being a part of the developer community in Austin. I cannot believe I get to wake up every day continuing to learn and create things that work, delight, and have a clean and beautiful design. It is a dream.

I am inspired by contemporary minimalist design, literary masters of Elizabethan England, and Color Field painting.

i like to create in multiple media
