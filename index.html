31 July 2014

Improv Added

We are excited to announce several additions to the B. Iden Payne Awards for the 2013-2014 season.

The council has been in conversation with the Austin improv community throughout the past year to discuss the addition of this vital and thriving part of our theatre community. We wanted to hear from them about how we might best honor their work in keeping with our established practices.

At their recommendation, the council has elected to add two new awards for Improv performance: Outstanding Production of Improv and Outstanding Direction of Improv. New artistic guidelines can be found on our page. In addition to these two awards, eligible productions will also be considered for all of our acting categories (including cast and ensemble) and general categories. The Rudy Kloptic Award for Outstanding Improv troupe, nominated by the improv community, will remain.

Also new this year, we are adding a general category for Stage Management. We all know just how much these individuals contribute to the success of a production, and we have long wanted to honor that.

We are so excited to bring these three new awards to the Austin theatre community and look forward to viewing these productions!
